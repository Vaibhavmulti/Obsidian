# 30-Day Gratitude & Self-Awareness Journaling Challenge

Time Required: 10–15 minutes daily  
Structure Each Day:

1. 3 Gratitudes (specific, not generic)
    
2. 1 Core Reflection Question (based on the day)
    
3. 1 Micro Intention for tomorrow
    

---

## Daily Template (Use Every Day)

**3 Gratitudes**  
1.  
2.  
3. 

**Core Reflection**

**Micro Intention for Tomorrow**  
→

---

# WEEK 1 (Days 1–7): Awareness

Goal: Observe yourself without judgment.

### Day 1 – Reality Check

- What does a normal day in my life actually look like?
    
- Where does my time really go?
    
- When do I feel most focused?
    
- When do I feel low energy?
    

### Day 2 – Energy Map

- What gives me energy?
    
- What drains me?
    
- Which people energize me?
    
- Which habits drain me?
    

### Day 3 – Triggers

- What irritated me today?
    
- Why did it affect me?
    
- What belief was underneath that reaction?
    

### Day 4 – Distractions

- What did I avoid today?
    
- What did I use as an escape?
    
- What am I not facing?
    

### Day 5 – Confidence Audit

- When did I feel confident?
    
- When did I feel small?
    
- What behavior increased confidence?
    

### Day 6 – Discipline Check

- Where did I keep promises to myself?
    
- Where did I break them?
    
- Why?
    

### Day 7 – Weekly Reflection

- Biggest insight this week?
    
- What pattern did I notice?
    
- What surprised me about myself?
    

---

# WEEK 2 (Days 8–14): Emotional Depth

### Day 8 – Fear

- What am I currently afraid of?
    
- What would happen if that fear came true?
    
- What is in my control?
    

### Day 9 – Comparison

- Who do I compare myself to?
    
- Why?
    
- What insecurity does that reveal?
    

### Day 10 – Anger

- What makes me angry?
    
- Is it actually anger, or hurt?
    

### Day 11 – Appreciation

- Who in my life deserves more appreciation?
    
- Did I show it?
    

### Day 12 – Childhood Influence

- What belief from childhood still influences me?
    
- Is it serving me?
    

### Day 13 – Ego Check

- Where do I need to be right?
    
- Where do I seek validation?
    

### Day 14 – Emotional Summary

- What emotion dominates my life right now?
    
- Why?
    

---

# WEEK 3 (Days 15–21): Identity & Direction

### Day 15 – Ideal Self

- Describe the man I want to be in 3 years.
    
- What habits does he have?
    
- How does he behave under pressure?
    

### Day 16 – Values

- What 5 values matter most to me?
    
- Do my actions reflect them?
    

### Day 17 – Time Honesty

- If someone observed me for 30 days, what would they say I care about?
    

### Day 18 – Strengths

- What am I naturally good at?
    
- Where do people seek my help?
    

### Day 19 – Weakness Patterns

- What recurring mistake keeps repeating?
    

### Day 20 – Relationship Depth

- Did I make my wife feel valued this week?
    
- How can I show more presence?
    

### Day 21 – Identity Gap

- What is the gap between who I am and who I want to be?
    
- What is one daily behavior that closes it?
    

---

# WEEK 4 (Days 22–30): Integration & Power

### Day 22 – Eliminate

- What must I remove from my life to grow?
    

### Day 23 – Build

- What habit will change my trajectory most?
    

### Day 24 – Courage

- What uncomfortable action would move me forward?
    

### Day 25 – Gratitude Expansion

Write 10 gratitudes instead of 3.

### Day 26 – Forgiveness

- Who do I need to forgive?
    
- Do I need to forgive myself?
    

### Day 27 – Money & Ambition

- What does financial success mean to me?
    
- Why do I want it?
    

### Day 28 – Mortality

- If I had 5 years left, what would change?
    

### Day 29 – Vision

Write one full page describing your life 3 years from now.

### Day 30 – Final Integration

- What changed in me this month?
    
- What surprised me?
    
- What will I continue daily?
    

---

## Weekly Deep Reflection (Optional – Every Sunday)

1. What worked this week?
    
2. What didn’t?
    
3. Where did I waste time?
    
4. What did I learn about myself?
    
5. What kind of person did I act like this week?
